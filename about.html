About This Game

Welcome to our Rock, Paper, Scissors game! This game is designed to provide a fun and interactive way to play the classic game of Rock, Paper, Scissors. Whether you're looking for a quick classic game or want to try the fun mode of rock paper scissors, this game has got you covered.

In the classic mode, you can play against the computer and test your skills. In the fun mode, you can enjoy a more playful version of the game with cute emojis representing each move. The game keeps track of your wins, losses, and ties, allowing you to see how well you're doing over time .

We hope you enjoy playing and have fun competing against the computer. Feel free to explore both modes and see which one you like best!

How to Play

Classic Mode

In the classic mode, you will play against the computer. To make your move, simply click on the corresponding button for rock, paper, or scissors. The computer will randomly select its move, and the game will determine the winner based on the traditional rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

After each round, the game will display the result and update your score. You can reset your score at any time by clicking the "Reset Score" button.

Fun Mode

In the fun mode, you can enjoy a more playful version of the game with cute emojis representing each move. The game will then determine the winner based on the traditional rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors:but here :

  • win=+1
  • loss=-1
  • tie=0
  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

After each round, the game will display the result and update your score. You can reset your score at any time by clicking the "Reset Score" button.
